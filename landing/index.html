THE THESIS

Fans create value.
They should capture it.

Every stream, every share, every early listen builds an artist's career. But fans never see the upside. LSTNR changes that.

The Problem

Music discovery is passive. Fans listen, stream, and share — but they never participate in the upside. The value is emotional. The capture is one-sided.

The Opportunity

A $28B+ global music industry with zero cultural-finance products. No one lets fans invest in artists like stocks. This is uncharted territory.

The Solution

LSTNR tokenizes artist momentum into tradable assets. Fans buy shares, predict outcomes, and earn when their artists rise. Culture becomes an asset class.

HOW IT WORKS

Three steps to invest in culture.

01

Discover

Browse emerging and trending artists. Analyze Market Confidence Scores, price history, and streaming momentum before you invest.

02

Invest

Buy artist shares and stake positions in prediction markets. Back the artists you believe in, or bet on specific cultural outcomes.

03

Earn

As artists grow, your shares appreciate. Win predictions, earn payouts. Join holders-only communities where real conviction unlocks access.
